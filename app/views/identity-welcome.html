Prove your identity in person

If you cannot prove your identity online with a GOV.UK account you can still continue to apply. You will still need your identity and financial documents to hand. But you’ll also need to go to a UK post office in person after completing your application.

You’ll have 10 days to take your identity documents to a UK post office that offers the DBS ID validation service.

We’ll only start processing your application after you’ve proven your identity at a UK post office.
