Prove your identity with a GOV.UK One Login

It will take you about 10 minutes to prove your identity this way.

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg). The GOV.UK ID Check app is currently only available in English.

You'll need:

  • a GOV.UK One Login - we’ll ask you to create or sign in to your One Login when you continue
  • your photo ID
  • your current home address (you might also need your previous address if you've recently moved)

We’ll only use the information you give us to make sure that you are who you say you are.
